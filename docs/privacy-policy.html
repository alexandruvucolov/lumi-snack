While using our App, we may collect certain information automatically, including:

We use third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) to display advertisements in the App. These services may collect and use information about your device and usage patterns to provide personalized ads. The information collected by AdMob is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

AdMob may collect:

For more information about Google AdMob's privacy practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Storage

We store the following data locally on your device:

4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information.

You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the App at any time

6. Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting any data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:
